## PRODUCT CONTEXT

Doculet.ai is a Nigerian bank statement verification platform. Students upload documents as supporting evidence for their Proof of Funds Certificate. Documents are critical to the trust score (20 points max) and certificate readiness.

The documents page manages 11 document types: Bank Statement, Admission Letter, Tuition Invoice, Passport, Visa, Transcript, Tax Return, Sponsor Letter, Certificate/Diploma, Scholarship Letter, Other. Each uploaded document goes through a verification pipeline: pending → scanning → verified OR needs_review OR rejected. Rejected documents show the rejection reason and a "Re-upload" button. Approved documents contribute to the trust score (+4-6 pts depending on type).

**Cross-persona handoff**: When a student uploads a document, an `admin_review_queue` entry is created (type: document_review). The admin reviews it in their queue → approves/rejects/requests info → the student receives a notification and sees the updated status badge. V5 gap: admin decisions didn't trigger notifications — V6 must wire this. Files are stored in Supabase Storage at `documents/{user_id}/{doc_type}/`, max 10MB, PDF/JPG/PNG only. All copy from `src/config/copy/student.ts`, amounts in ₦, timestamps in WAT.

## Design System Rules (MANDATORY)
- Semantic tokens ONLY: `bg-background`, `text-foreground`, `bg-primary` — NEVER raw Tailwind colors
- Dark mode: `dark:` variants on everything that has a background/text color
- Mobile-first: default = 375px, add `md:` and `lg:` for larger screens
- Touch targets: 44×44px minimum (WCAG 2.2)
- All copy from `src/config/copy/` — never hardcode strings in JSX
- Icons: Lucide only, nav=24px inline=20px status=16px
- Animations: `duration-150` micro, `duration-200` fast, `duration-300` normal
