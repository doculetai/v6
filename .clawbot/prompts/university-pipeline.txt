Build the university application pipeline at src/app/dashboard/university/pipeline/page.tsx (Server) + pipeline-page-client.tsx (Client).

## Page Purpose
University admissions officers review students who have selected their school/program and have funding verified.

## Pipeline View

A Kanban-style board with 4 columns (scroll horizontally on mobile, tabs on small screens):

| Pending Review | Under Review | Approved | Rejected |
|---------------|-------------|---------|---------|
| Student cards | Student cards | Student cards | Student cards |

Each student card shows:
- Student name + photo placeholder (initials avatar)
- Program applied to
- Funding tier badge (Tier 1 / Tier 2 / Tier 3)
- Funding amount (₦)
- Days in current status
- Quick action: Move to next status

## Alternative: List View (toggle button top-right)

Paginated table (20 per page):
| Student | Program | Funding Tier | Funding Amount | Status | Applied Date | Actions |

Mobile: stacked cards (NOT a table).

Status filter tabs: All | Pending | Under Review | Approved | Rejected | Enrolled

## Bulk Actions
Select mode (checkbox per card/row):
- Bulk Approve → confirmation modal → tRPC.university.bulkApprove(studentIds)
- Bulk Reject → modal with rejection reason field → tRPC.university.bulkReject(studentIds, reason)
- Export Selected → tRPC.university.exportCSV(studentIds) → CSV download

## Individual Actions (per card)
- Approve → tRPC.university.approveStudent(studentId) + triggers email via Resend
- Reject → modal: reason field → tRPC.university.rejectStudent(studentId, reason)
- Request Documents → tRPC.university.requestDocuments(studentId, message)
- View Full Profile → /dashboard/university/students/[studentId]

## CSV Export
tRPC.university.exportCSV() returns:
Student Name, Program, Funding Amount, Funding Tier, Status, Application Date, KYC Status, Sponsor Name

## Stats Bar (top of page)
Four stat chips: Total Applicants | Pending | Approved | Enrolled

## Search + Filter
- Search: by student name or email (client-side debounce)
- Filter: by program (dropdown), by funding tier (1/2/3)
