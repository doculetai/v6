## What Is An Agent?
An "agent" is a referral partner — an individual or company that refers students to Doculet in exchange for a commission per successful sponsorship.

## Page Layout

### EarningsPanel (Agent Variant)
Three stat cards:
- Total Commission Earned (₦): sum of all paid commissions
- Pending Commission (₦): sum of commissions awaiting payment
- Commission This Month (₦): for current calendar month

Below stats: a line chart showing commission earnings over last 12 months.

### Student Portfolio List

Table/card grid of all referred students:
| Student | School | Sponsor | Funding Amount | Commission Rate | Commission (₦) | Status |

Commission Rate is stored per-agent-student relationship (e.g., 2.5%).
Commission amount = Funding Amount × Commission Rate.
Status: pending (funding not yet disbursed) / earned (disbursed) / paid (paid to agent).

Mobile: stacked cards with key info.

**Sort:** by date added (default), by commission amount
**Filter:** by status (all/pending/earned/paid)

### Referral Link Generator

Card with:
- Agent's unique referral URL: `https://app.doculet.ai/signup?ref=[agentCode]`
- Copy button (copies to clipboard with toast feedback)
- Share button (Web Share API on mobile)
- Stats: "X students signed up via your link" + conversion rate

## Commission Detail (click on a student row)

Sheet/modal shows:
- Student name + school + program
- Sponsorship amount (₦)
- Commission rate (%)
- Commission due (₦)
- Commission status + payment date (if paid)
- Payment reference (if paid via Paystack)
